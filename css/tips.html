Paris

  • Visit the Eiffel Tower early to avoid long queues.
  • Buy a museum pass to save time and money.
  • Explore Montmartre for local art and cafes.

Tokyo

  • Get a Suica/Pasmo card for easy metro travel.
  • Try sushi at Tsukiji fish market.
  • Visit Shibuya Crossing at night.

Maldives

  • Book a water villa in advance.
  • Bring reef-safe sunscreen.
  • Take a snorkeling or diving trip.

Egypt

  • Wear light clothes but cover shoulders/knees at sites.
  • Hire a guide at the pyramids for history insights.
  • Take a Nile cruise for a unique perspective.

Rome

  • Book Colosseum tickets online in advance.
  • Wear comfy shoes for walking tours.
  • Try authentic gelato in local shops.

New York

  • Buy a MetroCard for subway travel.
  • Visit Central Park in the morning.
  • Book Broadway tickets early.

Top 5 Travel Tips

  1. Book your flights early to save money.
  2. Always keep digital copies of important documents.
  3. Learn a few basic phrases in the local language.
  4. Pack light and smart – carry only essentials.
  5. Respect local culture and traditions.
